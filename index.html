About Me

Hello

My name is Carlos. Born and raised in southern California. Graduated from UC San Diego with a B.S. in Aerospace Engineering. I was exposed to some programming in my undergrad by using Matlab and some C++ language to solve complex simulation problems. And, I love playing new games, especially boardgames. This is what lead me towards programming. I love being able to make a program to enhance the experience of a board game for myself and others. In the end, I just want improve user experience that make it fun to use.

Don't get me wrong, I still love the aerospace industry. I've always been fascinated by planes, rockets, and space. I just equally love technology and how much it can improve our daily lives. That's why want to expand to my skill set so I can constantly pursue my passions.
